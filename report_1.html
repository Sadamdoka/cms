Note!

Please register your complaint online and we will get the required information. Several officers access and collaborate on cases instead of relying on one officer via WhatsApp or phone call. This reduces the time spent to re-register complaints on the system after receiving them on WhatsApp or through phone calls. Our Officers can focus on resolving submitted cases instead of registering complaints.
We triage all cases and action priority is given based on emergency, importance, and capacity. But we ensure that all cases are handled and thus a case ticket number is issued for every complaint registered. If a case ticket number is not provided within 48 working hours, please send a text message to +966567826817. Please be advised that this number will only attend to inquiries about the case ticket number. Audio messages, video messages, and chat messages to this number will NOT be attended to.
You may not be aware but we serve many Ugandans, and the way we receive your information helps us to serve you better as well as other Ugandans. Incomplete information, chatting, audios, videos, and picture methods of communication to various parties hinder our turnaround time in resolving cases due to the delays faced when analyzing this content and in the long run other Ugandans are denied an opportunity of service.
We continue doing our best to improve our services.
