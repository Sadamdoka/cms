CEEMIS

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4 March 2025

The Ministry of Gender, Labour, and Social Development of the Government of Uganda ("Ministry", "we", "us") is fully committed to protecting the privacy, dignity, and rights of all individuals who use the Complaints External Employment Management Information System ("CEEMIS").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share personal data when you access or use CEEMIS, including the CEEMIS Migrant Worker mobile app and CEEMIS Officer portal.

  • Migrant workers accessing the CEEMIS Migrant Worker App
  • Case officers and Ministry personnel using the CEEMIS Officer portal
  • Visitors or users interacting with CEEMIS via web or mobile

This Policy ensures compliance with national data protection laws and international best practices.

A. Migrant Workers
  • Identification: Name, DOB, nationality, contact, passport, ID
  • Employment: Job title, agency, employer, contract, work history
  • Complaints: Case ID, type, documents, logs
  • Sensitive Info: Medical, financial, legal info (only if needed)
B. Officers
  • Full name, position, department, contacts, login logs
  • Case activity logs, audit trails, communication with workers
C. General Users
  • Support contacts (if submitted), messages
  • Device info: IP, browser, access logs, crash reports
  • Support Migrant Workers: Register complaints, send updates, legal aid
  • Officer Empowerment: Transparent resolution, generate reports
  • System Improvements: Analyze trends, improve security and policy planning
  • Consent: Given during registration or complaint submission
  • Public Task: Mandate of the Ministry
  • Legal Obligation: National and international compliance
  • Legitimate Interests: Improve CEEMIS, prevent abuse

Data is shared only as required:

  • With your consent
  • With authorized officers
  • With legal authorities when necessary
  • With service providers under strict agreements
  • Active cases: Stored until resolved
  • Closed cases: Retained for up to 10 years
  • Archived data: May be anonymized for research/statistics
  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access control based on roles
  • Regular vulnerability assessments
  • Incident response protocols

CEEMIS stores data in Uganda. Any international transfers comply with data protection laws and proper safeguards.

  • Access, rectify, or delete your data
  • Request processing restrictions
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent

Contact us to exercise your rights.

CEEMIS does not knowingly collect data from children under 18 without parental consent. Such data will be deleted if discovered.
